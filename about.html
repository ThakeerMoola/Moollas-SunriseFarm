About Us

Our Story

Three siblings spurred on by a long line of farmers that span more than 3 generations dating back to 1980. Raised, supported and motivated by a strong independent mother with traditional family values and a fierce work ethic, a dream emerged, a dream we are working to make a reality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build long-term relationships with clients, other farmers, employees and other businesses within our community. We believe this mission will only be possible by providing the best quality animals in a world class facility


AMFA

We are affiliated to the African Muslim Farmers Association (AMFA) and are committed to upholding the stringent Shariah laws under which our animals are grown under the highest standards of quality, using ethical procedures in a natural environment.

Our Farm

Our farm based in the heart of Johannesburg South (Eikenhof) is a welcoming place for families to gather and interact with each other as well as with our animals. A place to connect with nature and have incredible animal encounters.
